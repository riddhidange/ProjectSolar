MANAGER

Project Manager determines goals, objectives, and scope of the project. The Manager also developes a project outline, keep track of activities, deadlines, and resources needed. To keep the project on schedule, they need maintain communication with the project team and key stakeholders, manage risks and concerns, and keep an eye on development which is provided to them via our tool.

SALES TEAM

Sales representatives serves as the company's primary point of contact with prospects and clients. They are responsible for a variety of tasks, such as locating and educating potential consumers as well as providing current clients with information and support regarding goods and services. This platform provides them a portal to get in contact with the team and customers.

ENGINEERS

The Engineers team is in charge of carrying out the actual building and manufacture of the specified good or service. All of the sprints are run by them, and they are also responsible for developing any new or essential features, upgrades, and repairs. This tool provides them a platform to update the changes made to projects, and also get in contact with the team.

CUSTOMERS

Customer is an individual or a corporation that purchases products or services from the company. This tool provides them access to the development of their purchases and also the means to get in contact with the team and share their concerns and feedback.
